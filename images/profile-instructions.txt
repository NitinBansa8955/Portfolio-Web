PROFILE IMAGE SETUP
==================

Your portfolio is looking for: images/profile.jpg

OPTION 1: Use Your Own Photo
-----------------------------
1. Take a professional headshot (square format preferred)
2. Save it as 'profile.jpg' in this images/ folder
3. Recommended size: 500x500px or larger (will be auto-resized)

OPTION 2: Generate a Placeholder
---------------------------------
Use an online avatar generator:
- https://ui-avatars.com/api/?name=Nitin+Bansal&size=500&background=2c3e50&color=fff&bold=true
- Right-click the generated image ? Save as 'profile.jpg' in this folder

OPTION 3: Use LinkedIn/GitHub Photo
------------------------------------
Download your profile picture from LinkedIn or GitHub and save as 'profile.jpg'

Until you add a real photo, the page will show a broken image icon.
