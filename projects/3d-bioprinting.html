3D-Bioprinting Parameters Optimization

Advisor: Prof. Prachi Thareja, IIT Gandhinagar

January 2025 - April 2025

Overview

Developed a real-time predictive ML model (Random Forest) that recommends optimal printing temperature and pressure based on CAD design complexity and bioink (Nivea Cream).

Technical Highlights

  • Feature extraction from CAD: Implemented a Bresenham Intersection Complexity algorithm (MATLAB) to quantify geometric complexity from CAD files.
  • Modeling: Random Forest trained on experimental data with features from CAD and material properties.
  • Validation: Multiple hands-on trials in the lab comparing predicted settings to actual print quality.

Results

The ML-predicted temperature and pressure settings achieved ~80% similarity with actual print quality across multiple design complexities.
